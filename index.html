// FAQ DATABASE

Does the design include Frontend Coding?

Our Standard and Pro packages focus on UI/UX Design (Figma files). However, for the Enterprise plan, we provide full *Developer Handoff* documentation or can include React/Vue implementation for an additional fee.

What is the estimated turnaround time?

For MVP projects (3-5 screens), it typically takes 3-5 business days. For full trading platforms (20+ screens), it may take 2-4 weeks depending on the complexity of the features.

Is my trading strategy data secure?

Strictly confidential. We are ready to sign an NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement) before the project begins to guarantee the safety of your algorithms and business strategies.
